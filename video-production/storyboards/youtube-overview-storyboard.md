## Storyboard Overview

This is the THOROUGH version optimized for YouTube. Viewers chose to watch, so we can go deeper.

**Structure:**
1. **HOOK** (0-7s) - Relatable problem statement
2. **PROBLEM + STAKES** (7-18s) - Establish emotional and practical consequences
3. **POSITIONING** (18-26s) - Introduce Run-Smart as the solution
4. **STEP 1: Goal Selection** (26-38s) - Onboarding walkthrough
5. **STEP 2: Today & Calendar** (38-52s) - Core interface overview
6. **STEP 3: Magic Moment** (52-72s) - Adaptation deep dive ‚≠ê
7. **STEP 4: Coaching + Recovery** (72-86s) - AI and recovery features
8. **RESULT + CTA** (86-95s) - Clear booking invitation

**Key Differences from Landing Page:**
- Slower, more educational pace
- Slightly longer explanations
- More time to absorb each screen
- Optional B-roll moments (minimal)
- YouTube-specific formatting (chapters, timestamps)

## Overall Video Specifications

### Visual Style
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920√ó1080)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **UI Coverage:** 85-95% of frame
- **Color Grading:** Vibrant, optimistic, professional
- **B-roll:** OPTIONAL, maximum 5-10s total (5-10% of runtime)

### Audio
- **Voiceover:** Educational, thoughtful, approachable expert
- **Speaking Rate:** ~135-145 words per minute (slower than Landing Page)
- **Music:** Background only, VERY low volume (-20dB below VO)
- **Music Style:** Calm, modern, slightly uplifting (Epidemic Sound, Artlist)

### Text Overlays
- **Font:** Professional sans-serif (Inter, Proxima Nova, Helvetica)
- **Color:** White text with subtle shadow OR brand colors
- **Size:** Readable but not overwhelming
- **Animation:** Simple fade in/out (0.5s)
- **Duration:** 4-8s per overlay (longer than Landing Page)
- **Style:** Clean, professional, not "social media flashy"

### Transitions
- **Between Screens:** Fade (0.5s)
- **Within Segments:** Smooth, minimal
- **Magic Moment:** Highlight box + zoom (as specified)
- **End Card:** Fade in (1s)

### Pacing
- **Overall Feel:** Educational, thorough, not rushed
- **Cut Frequency:** 1 cut every 6-10s (chapter-like pacing)
- **Pauses:** Built into VO and editing for comprehension
- **Viewer Retention:** Educational value throughout, payoff at 52-72s
