## Storyboard Overview

This is the FAST version optimized for social media. Every second counts.

**Structure:**
1. **HOOK** (0-4s) - Stop the scroll immediately
2. **SETUP** (4-10s) - Quick explanation of solution
3. **START** (10-16s) - Super fast onboarding + today view
4. **MAGIC MOMENT** (16-24s) - Core adaptation demo
5. **CTA** (24-30s) - Clear booking call-to-action

**Key Differences from Landing Page:**
- 3x faster pacing
- Only shows adaptation feature (not full workflow)
- Bold, punchy text overlays
- Designed for sound-OFF viewing (captions critical)
- Optimized for mobile vertical viewing

## Overall Video Specifications

### Visual Style
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (1080√ó1920)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (or 60fps for smoother feel on social)
- **UI Coverage:** 90-95% of frame (mobile-first, no wasted space)
- **Color Grading:** High contrast, vibrant (pops on mobile screens)

### Audio
- **Voiceover:** Fast-paced, energetic, relatable
- **Music:** High energy, modern, royalty-free
- **Music Volume:** Louder than landing page (music helps retention)
- **Audio Mix:** -12dB music under VO (vs -18dB for landing page)

### Text Overlays
- **Font:** VERY BOLD sans-serif (e.g., Impact, Anton, Montserrat Black)
- **Color:** White text with dark stroke/shadow (maximum readability)
- **Size:** LARGE (20-30% of screen height)
- **Animation:** Pop/bounce in (energetic)
- **Duration:** 2-4s per overlay (faster than landing page)
- **Style:** TikTok/Reel native look (not corporate)

### Captions (CRITICAL)
- **MUST have captions** (80% of social views are sound-off)
- **Auto-generated captions** via editing software (Premiere, CapCut)
- **Style:** Word-by-word highlight (karaoke style) OR full sentence
- **Position:** Bottom third (don't block UI)
- **Color:** Yellow or white with black outline

### Transitions
- **Between Screens:** Quick cuts (no fade) OR fast wipe (0.2s)
- **Magic Moment:** Quick fade or swipe (0.3s max)
- **End Card:** Fade in (0.5s)

### Pacing
- **Overall Feel:** FAST, energetic, scroll-stopping
- **Cut Frequency:** 1 cut every 2-4s
- **No Pauses:** Continuous motion and energy
- **Viewer Retention:** Hook in first 1 second, payoff at 16-24s
