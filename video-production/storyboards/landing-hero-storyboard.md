# Landing Hero Storyboard (16:9, 80s)

**Format:** 16:9 Desktop/Web
**Target Duration:** 80 seconds
**Purpose:** Primary conversion tool for website visitors → demo bookings
**Audience:** All personas (customize intro/CTA per persona variant)

---

## Storyboard Overview

This storyboard maps the complete 80-second Landing Hero video from voiceover to visuals to editing notes.

**Structure:**
1. **HOOK** (0-6s) - Grab attention with relatable problem
2. **PROBLEM** (6-14s) - Establish pain points
3. **POSITIONING** (14-20s) - Introduce Run-Smart as solution
4. **STEP 1: Get Started** (20-30s) - Onboarding
5. **STEP 2: Plan Appears** (30-42s) - Today + Calendar views
6. **STEP 3: Magic Moment** (42-58s) - Adaptation showcase ⭐
7. **STEP 4: Coaching + Recovery** (58-70s) - AI chat + recovery
8. **RESULT + CTA** (70-80s) - End card with booking CTA

---

### SEGMENT 6: STEP 3 — MAGIC MOMENT (42-58s) ⭐ CRITICAL

| Time | Voiceover | Visual | On-Screen Text | Edit Notes |
|------|-----------|--------|----------------|------------|
| **42-58s** | "Miss a run? No guilt. Run-Smart adjusts the plan automatically, so you keep momentum instead of starting over." | **[SCREEN: Missed Run] → [SCREEN: Adapted Plan]**<br><br>**42-46s:** Missed workout visible<br>- Calendar showing yesterday's missed workout<br>- Clear "missed" indicator<br><br>**46-48s:** Trigger adaptation<br>- "Update Plan" button OR "Overnight" transition<br>- Brief loading (0.5-1s)<br><br>**48-58s:** Adapted plan revealed<br>- Updated workouts visible<br>- Visual indicator of change (highlight, badge)<br>- Pause to let viewer see the changes | "No guilt. Auto-adjusted." (appears at 50s after adaptation completes) | **Pacing:** SLOW down here—this is the key moment<br>**Music:** Builds slightly, then resolves<br>**Zoom:** Big zoom (110%) on updated day at 50s<br>**Highlight:** Box or glow around changed workouts<br>**Voiceover:** Pause after "No guilt." for impact<br>**Goal:** Make the adaptation OBVIOUS and satisfying |

**Visual Details:**

**Before State (42-46s):**
- Calendar view with 1-2 missed workouts
- "Missed" badge clear and visible (red or gray)
- Date context (e.g., "Yesterday" or "Tuesday")
- Don't show too many missed workouts (implies failure)

**Transition (46-48s):**

*Option A: Button Trigger*
- "Update Plan" or "Refresh Plan" button visible
- Cursor clicks button
- Brief loading spinner (0.5-1s max)

*Option B: Overnight Simulation*
- "Overnight" overlay appears (moon icon, "Next morning")
- Time-lapse effect (2s)
- Implies automatic adaptation

**After State (48-58s):**
- Same calendar view but updated
- Workouts rearranged logically:
  - Example: Long run moved from midweek to weekend
  - Rest day inserted where needed
  - Future workouts adjusted for progressive overload
- Visual callout: "Updated" badge or green highlight
- Hold for 8-10 seconds to let viewer see changes

**Critical Success Factors:**
- Before/after difference is IMMEDIATELY OBVIOUS
- Updated plan makes logical sense (not random)
- Visual feedback is satisfying (animation, color, badge)
- Viewer has time to process what happened (don't rush)

**Zoom Effect:**
- At 50s, zoom to 110% on the updated day
- Hold zoom for 2s
- Zoom back out at 52s
- This is the biggest zoom in the video—emphasize the magic

**Text Overlay Style:**
- Appears at 50s (after adaptation completes)
- Larger and bolder than previous overlays
- Bright color (green or brand color)
- Holds through 58s

**Additional Edit Notes:**
- Consider subtle animation on updated workouts (fade in, slide in)
- Sound effect on adaptation completion (optional, subtle chime)
- This segment should feel satisfying and magical

## Overall Video Specifications

### Visual Style
- **Color Grading:** Bright, vibrant, optimistic
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920×1080)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **UI Coverage:** 85-95% of frame (UI is the star)
- **Padding:** 5% margin on all sides (nothing cut off)

### Audio
- **Voiceover:** Professional, conversational, warm
- **Music:** Background only, low volume (-18dB below VO)
- **Music Style:** Calm, modern, slightly uplifting
- **Music Source:** Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or royalty-free

### Text Overlays
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Proxima Nova, Helvetica)
- **Color:** White text with 60% black background OR brand colors
- **Size:** Large enough to read on mobile
- **Animation:** Simple fade in/out (0.3s)
- **Duration:** 3-5s per overlay

### Transitions
- **Between Screens:** Fade (0.3-0.5s)
- **Within Montage:** Quick cut (no transition)
- **Magic Moment:** Highlight box + zoom (as specified)

### Pacing
- **Overall Feel:** Calm but engaging, not rushed
- **Cut Frequency:** 1 cut every 5-8s (except HOOK montage)
- **Pauses:** Built into VO and editing
- **Viewer Retention:** Hook in first 3s, payoff at 42-58s (magic moment)
