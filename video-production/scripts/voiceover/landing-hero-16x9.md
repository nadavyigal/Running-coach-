# Landing Page Hero Voiceover Script (16:9, 80s)

**Format:** 16:9 Desktop/Web
**Duration:** 80 seconds
**Purpose:** Primary conversion tool for website visitors
**Tone:** Empathetic, solution-focused, professional but warm

---

## Time-Coded Voiceover Script

### HOOK (0–6s)
> "Ever start a running plan… then miss one day and feel like you failed? Run-Smart is the AI running coach that adapts when life happens."

**Pacing:** Conversational pause after "plan…"
**Emphasis:** "adapts when life happens"

---

### PROBLEM (6–14s)
> "Most plans assume a perfect schedule. Real life doesn't. That's why people quit by week two or three."

**Pacing:** Brief pause after "Real life doesn't."
**Emphasis:** "quit by week two or three" (the pain point)

---

### POSITIONING (14–20s)
> "Run-Smart helps you build a running habit with adaptive training, simple recovery guidance, and coaching that actually listens."

**Pacing:** Even, confident
**Emphasis:** "adaptive training" and "actually listens"

---

### STEP 1 — GET STARTED (20–30s)
> "Pick your goal and your experience level. Run-Smart sets a plan that fits your week."

**Pacing:** Clear, instructional
**Emphasis:** "fits your week"

---

### STEP 2 — PLAN APPEARS (30–42s)
> "You'll see today's workout and your week at a glance—clear, simple, doable."

**Pacing:** Calm, reassuring
**Emphasis:** "clear, simple, doable" (rhythm of three)

---

### STEP 3 — MAGIC MOMENT: ADAPTATION (42–58s)
> "Miss a run? No guilt. Run-Smart adjusts the plan automatically, so you keep momentum instead of starting over."

**Pacing:** Pause after "No guilt." for impact
**Emphasis:** "adjusts the plan automatically" and "keep momentum"
**Note:** This is the core value proposition—deliver with confidence

---

### STEP 4 — COACHING + RECOVERY (58–70s)
> "Ask the coach anything—'Should I run today?'—and get a quick, practical answer based on your plan and how you're feeling."

**Pacing:** Natural, conversational
**Emphasis:** "quick, practical answer"
**Note:** The question should sound like a genuine user query

---

### RESULT + CTA (70–80s)
> "Your next workout is ready, right when you are. Book a 15-minute walkthrough and I'll show you how Run-Smart fits your life."

**Pacing:** Confident close
**Emphasis:** "Book a 15-minute walkthrough"
**Note:** The CTA should feel like an invitation, not a hard sell

---

## Delivery Notes

### Overall Tone
- Empathetic but not overly emotional
- Professional but warm and human
- Solution-focused, not problem-dwelling
- Conversational, not salesy

### Key Moments
1. **Hook (0-6s):** Must grab attention immediately—deliver the question with genuine curiosity
2. **Magic Moment (42-58s):** This is the core differentiator—slow down slightly and emphasize confidence
3. **CTA (70-80s):** Invitational tone, like you're offering to help a friend

### Pacing Guidelines
- Average speaking rate: ~140-150 words per minute
- Pause points marked in script
- Don't rush the CTA—it needs to feel natural

### Technical Specs
- Record in a quiet environment
- Use a quality microphone (USB condenser minimum)
- Aim for -18dB to -12dB average loudness
- Record multiple takes of the CTA and magic moment
