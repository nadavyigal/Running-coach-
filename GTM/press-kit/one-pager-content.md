## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Emerald Green (#10b981)
- **Secondary:** Dark Gray (#1f2937)
- **Accent:** White (#ffffff)
- **Background:** Light Gray (#f9fafb)

### Typography Hierarchy
- **H1 (Tagline):** 32pt, Bold
- **H2 (Section Headers):** 18pt, Bold
- **Body:** 11pt, Regular
- **Stats/Numbers:** 24pt, Bold
- **Captions:** 9pt, Regular

### Visual Elements
- **Icons:** Simple line icons (Lucide or Heroicons style)
- **Charts:** Minimal, 2-color max
- **Screenshots:** 1-2 app screenshots with phone mockup
- **Borders:** 1px emerald green for boxes
- **Shadows:** Subtle drop shadows for depth
- **White Space:** Generous padding, avoid cramping

### Layout Grid
- **3-column layout** with flexible widths
- **Boxes with rounded corners** (8px radius)
- **Consistent spacing** (16px between elements)
- **Visual hierarchy** using size + color + position
