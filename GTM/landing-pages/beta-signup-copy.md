## A/B Testing Ideas

   - Test A: "Be Among the First" (exclusivity)
   - Test B: "Get Lifetime 50% Off" (savings)
   - Test C: "Help Shape the Future" (collaboration)

2. **Form Length:**
   - Test A: Email only (minimal friction)
   - Test B: Email + Experience level (qualification)
   - Test C: Full form with goals (as above)

   - Test A: "Join the Beta Waitlist"
   - Test B: "Get Early Access"
   - Test C: "Reserve My Spot"

   - Test A: Countdown timer (beta starts in X days)

   - Test C: No urgency (trust-based)

## Conversion Optimization Notes

**Key goals:**
1. Maximize email captures (primary KPI)
2. Collect qualification data (experience, goals)
3. Create excitement and urgency
4. Set expectations for timeline
5. Encourage social sharing (viral loop)

**Trust builders:**
- Founder-led narrative (personal connection)
- Transparent about timeline and process
- No credit card required
- Clear benefits and requirements
- Real community stats

**Friction reducers:**
- Simple form (4-5 fields max)
- Optional fields clearly marked
- Mobile-optimized form
- Instant confirmation (no email verification to join waitlist)
- Progress indication (you're #X)

**Viral mechanisms:**
- Referral link on thank you page
- Social sharing buttons
- Incentive to share (move up waitlist)
- Pre-written social posts
