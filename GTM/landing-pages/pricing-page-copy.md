## Conversion Optimization Notes

**Key psychological principles:**
1. **Anchoring**: Show annual savings vs monthly
2. **Social proof**: "Most popular" badge on Premium
3. **Loss aversion**: "Beta pricing ending soon"
4. **Risk reversal**: Money-back guarantee
5. **Scarcity**: Limited beta spots
6. **Comparison**: Free vs Premium, Us vs Competitors

**Mobile-first design:**
- Sticky header with price comparison
- Collapsible feature table
- Fixed bottom CTA bar
- Easy toggle between monthly/annual

**Conversion tactics:**
- Free tier CTA less prominent (but still visible)
- Premium CTA uses high-contrast color
- Beta banner at top for urgency
- FAQ addresses objections directly
- Testimonials build trust
