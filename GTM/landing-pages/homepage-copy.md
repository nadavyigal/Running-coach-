## Conversion Optimization Notes

**Above the fold priorities:**
1. Clear value proposition (headline)
2. Immediate CTA (Join Beta)
3. Social proof (200+ runners)
4. Visual demo (app screenshot or video)

**Psychological triggers:**
- Problem-solution framework (identify with frustration)
- Social proof (others like you are doing this)
- Scarcity (beta access, early bird benefits)
- Authority (AI-powered, science-backed)
- Privacy (data control in sensitive health space)

**Mobile-first design:**
- Hero CTA above fold on mobile
- Feature cards scroll horizontally on small screens
- Video thumbnail (autoplay on mobile data warning)
- Sticky footer CTA on scroll

1. Headline: "Adapt to Your Life" vs "Build Habits That Stick" vs "Never Quit Running Again"
