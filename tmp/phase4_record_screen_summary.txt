   ---------------------------------------------------------------
   BEFORE: GPSAccuracyIndicator component with percentage
   AFTER:  Visual signal bars (5 bars, ascending height)

   Bar Design:
   • 5 bars with heights: 8px, 12px, 16px, 20px, 24px
   • Width: 1.5 (6px)
   • Rounded full
   • Animated transitions (300ms)

   Color Coding:
   • Excellent (80%+):  Green with cyan glow (bg-green-400 glow-cyan)
   • Good (60-79%):     Amber (bg-amber-400)

   • Percentage display next to bars
   • Accuracy radius below (±Xm)
   • Quality label (EXCELLENT/GOOD/FAIR/POOR)
