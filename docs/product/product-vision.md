# Product Vision

RunSmart Web helps everyday runners train smarter with an AI coach that turns plan data, activity history, readiness, recovery, and personal context into clear daily guidance.

## Vision
Make the runner feel calmly guided: "I know what to do today, why it matters, and how to adjust if life or my body changes the plan."

## Experience Pillars
- Today is the daily command center.
- Next best action is always obvious.
- Progress is visual and motivating, not a wall of numbers.
- AI coaching explains tradeoffs in plain language.
- Recovery and readiness are first-class, not afterthoughts.
- Integrations should reduce manual work, not add dashboard clutter.

## Direction
- Keep the web app mobile-first and PWA-friendly.
- Maintain compatibility with a future native iOS app.
- Design data models and UX for future Garmin and Apple Health data.
- Build trust through transparent guidance, safe defaults, and respectful uncertainty.

## Non-Goals
- Do not turn RunSmart into a generic fitness dashboard.
- Do not overload users with every possible metric.
- Do not make AI feel magical when a simple rule or clear explanation is better.
- Do not break existing training, auth, sync, or recording flows during visual improvements.
